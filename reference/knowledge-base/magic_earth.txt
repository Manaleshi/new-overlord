1st earth magics [eart]: The element of the Earth relates to growth, nature
  and animals. Requires 15 days and $3/day to reach the level. Only leaders
  may study this skill. Requires 1st magecraft [mage] to learn. Gives +1 to
  maximum mana.

1st strength of the boar [sboa]: During combat, the caster may inflict
  multiple hits to his foes and resist punishment. Requires 15 days and
  $3/day to reach the level. Only leaders may study this skill. Requires 1st
  earth magics [eart] to learn. This is a magic skill. Use requires: 2 mana
  [mana]. Gives +1 to maximum mana. This is a combat action. Initiative +1.
  Target: opposing unit. Range: melee. Effect: melee attack with modifiers
  +5 melee, +4 hits, +4 life.

1st enchant ring of strength [erst]: The caster uses his knowledge of
  earth's strength to enchant a lesser ring. Requires 15 days and $3/day to
  reach the level. Only leaders may study this skill. Requires 1st strength
  of the boar [sboa] to learn. This is a magic skill. Use requires: 1 iron
  ring [irng], 6 mana [mana]. Use produces 1 ring of strength [rtst] in 15
  days. Gives +1 to maximum mana.

1st floraison [flor]: While the mage concentrates, all units harvesting live
  products in the same region will produce an additional 50% products. Takes
  effect only on units appearing after the mage in the arrival order.
  Requires 15 days and $3/day to reach the level. Only leaders may study
  this skill. Requires 1st earth magics [eart] to learn. This is a magic
  skill. Use requires: 1 mana [mana]. Gives +1 to maximum mana.

1st gaia transformation [gaet]: The user of this spell calls forth the earth
  spirit itself, pledging itself for earth. The mage is transformed upon
  this into an avatar of gaea, a special life form, unmistakable. The mage
  will forget skills of the air and water elements at the beginning of each
  turn, randomly. Requires 30 days and $3/day to reach the level. Only
  leaders may study this skill. This is a specialist skill. Requires 1st
  earth magics [eart] to learn. This is a magic skill. Use requires: 1 grain
  [grai], 1 wood [wood], 1 herbs [herb], 1 stone [ston], 15 mana [mana]. Use
  produces 1 metamorphosis in 30 days. Gives +1 to maximum mana.
