1st magecraft [mage]: The basic magic skill. Knowledge of 1st level allows
  to study one element. Requires 15 days and $3/day to reach the level. Only
  leaders may study this skill. This is a basic skill. This is a magic
  skill. Gives +1 to maximum mana.

2nd magecraft [mage]: Advanced magic abilities. Knowledge of 2nd level
  allows to study a second element. Requires 45 days and $3/day to reach the
  level. Only leaders may study this skill. This is a magic skill. Gives +2
  to maximum mana.

3rd magecraft [mage]: Experienced magics. Knowledge of 3rd level allows to
  study a third element. Requires 90 days and $3/day to reach the level.
  Only leaders may study this skill. This is a magic skill. Gives +3 to
  maximum mana.

1st meditation [medi]: Meditation allows the mage to regain mana faster, and
  supercharge before major magics. Requires 15 days and $3/day to reach the
  level. Only leaders may study this skill. Requires 1st magecraft [mage] to
  learn. This is a magic skill. Use requires: 1 herbs [herb]. Use produces 1
  mana [mana] per day. Gives +1 to maximum mana.

2nd meditation [medi]: At this level, the mage gains mana faster than
  ever. Requires 45 days and $3/day to reach the level. Only leaders may
  study this skill. This is a magic skill. Use requires: 1 herbs [herb]. Use
  produces 2 mana [mana] per day. Gives +2 to maximum mana.

1st ritual [ritu]: By joining in a ritual, the mage lends its mana to the
  focus of a major spell. Requires 30 days and $3/day to reach the level.
  Only leaders may study this skill. Requires 1st meditation [medi] to
  learn. This is a magic skill. Use targets a unit. Use requires: 1 mana
  [mana]. Use produces 1 mana [mana] per day. Gives +1 to maximum mana.

2nd ritual [ritu]: At this level, a mage transfers double amount of mana per
  day. Requires 90 days and $3/day to reach the level. Only leaders may
  study this skill. This is a magic skill. Use targets a unit. Use requires:
  2 mana [mana]. Use produces 2 mana [mana] per day. Gives +1 to maximum
  mana.

1st make mage keys [mkky]: Creates a mage key out of precious metals.
  Requires 15 days and $3/day to reach the level. Only leaders may study
  this skill. Requires 2nd magecraft [mage] to learn. This is a magic skill.
  Use requires: 10 mana [mana], 1 silver [silv]. Use produces 1 mage key
  [mkey] in 15 days. Gives +1 to maximum mana.
