MAGECRAFT 
 
1st magecraft [mage]: The basic magic skill. Knowledge  
of 1st level allows the mage to study one element. Requires  
15 days and $3/day to reach the level. Only  leaders may  
study this skill. This is a basic skill. This is a magic skill. 
Gives +1 to maximum mana. 
  
  1st meditation [medi]: Meditation allows the mage to  
  regain mana faster, and  supercharge before major magics. 
  Requires 15 days and $3/day to reach the  level. Only  
  leaders may study this skill. Requires 1st magecraft [mage]  
  to learn. This is a magic skill. Use requires: 1 herbs  
  [herb]. Use produces 1 mana [mana] per day. Gives +1 to  
  maximum mana. 
  
    2nd meditation [medi]: At this level, the mage gains mana  
    faster than ever. Requires 45 days and $3/day to reach the  
    level. Only leaders may study this skill. This is a magic  
    skill. Use requires: 1 herbs [herb]. Use produces 2 mana  
    [mana] per day. Gives +2 to maximum mana. 
  
    1st ritual [ritu]: By joining in a ritual, the mage lends its  
    mana to the focus of a major spell. Requires 30 days and  
    $3/day to reach the level. Only leaders may study this skill.  
    Requires 1st meditation [medi] to learn. This is a magic  
    skill. Use targets a unit. Use requires: 1 mana [mana]. Use  
    produces 1 mana [mana] per day. Gives +1 to maximum  
    mana. 
  
2nd magecraft [mage]: Advanced magic abilities.  
Knowledge of 2nd level allows the mage to study a second  
element. Requires 45 days and $3/day to reach the level.  
Only leaders may study this skill. This is a magic skill.  
Gives +2  to maximum mana. 
  
  1st make mage keys [mkky]: Creates a mage key out of  
  precious metals. Requires 15 days and $3/day to reach the  
  level. Only leaders may study this skill. Requires 2nd  
  magecraft [mage] to learn. This is a magic skill. Use  
  requires: 10 mana [mana], 1 silver [silv]. Use produces 1  
  mage key [mkey] in 15 days. Gives +1 to maximum mana. 

    1st consecrate magical tower [ctow]: Only a mage can put 
    the finishing touch to a mage tower. Proper tradition has it 
    that the key is the last element added to a mage guild. 
    Requires 15 days and $1/day to reach the level. Requires 
    1st make mage keys [mkky] to learn. Use targets a structure. 
    Use requires: 1 mage key [mkey]. Use produces 1 magical 
    consecration per day.

    2nd make mage keys [mkky]: At this level, creating a mage 
    key is a lot easier. Requires 45 days and $3/day to reach the 
    level. Only leaders may study this skill. This is a magic skill. 
    Use requires: 10 mana [mana], 1 silver [silv]. Use produces 
    1 mage key [mkey] in 8 days. Gives +2 to maximum mana.
  
3rd magecraft [mage]: Experienced magics. Knowledge of  
3rd level allows the mage to study a third element. Requires  
90 days and $3/day to reach the level. Only leaders may  
study this skill. This is a magic skill. Gives +3 to maximum  
mana.
